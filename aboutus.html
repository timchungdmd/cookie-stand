History of our irresistable cookie recipe....

In the sweets and desserts world, cookies are considered especially heinous. In Seattle, the dedicated patisserie who bake these delicious cookie are members of an elite squad known as the Salmon Cookie Stand Unit. These are their stories......

It was year 1929....it was few days after the fateful day.....Black Tuesday.......worst economic collapse mankind has seen since the collapse of King Leonidas and his brave spartan soldiers.....

My great great grandfather was came back from an unfruitful salmon season to find out we lost everythingn in the stock market....

With nothing but despair looming over 4 generations living under one roof giving up was not an option

That's when great great grandfather came up with the idea of selling salmon flavored cookies

He was clearly drunk when he came up with that idea

But anyhow, my great great grandma was cooking shart shaped cooikies and the drunk great great grandpa said "Hey that looks like the salmon that slapped my face and escaped back to the sea!

...and that's how our famous salmoon cookie business started.
